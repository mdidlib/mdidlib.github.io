Module 03

Academic Integrity & Citations

Both tracks cover why we cite, how to avoid plagiarism, and how to cite AI tools. Choose the citation format you'll use for your IB research project.

MLA

MLA Format

Modern Language Association

Used in humanities subjects like English, History, Philosophy, and Arts.

Example citation:

Smith, John. "Article Title." Journal Name, vol. 12, no. 3, 2024, pp. 45-67.

Choose MLA
APA

APA Format

American Psychological Association

Used in sciences and social sciences like Psychology, Biology, and Economics.

Example citation:

Smith, J. (2024). Article title. Journal Name, 12(3), 45-67.

Choose APA

Not sure which to choose? Ask your teacher or supervisor which format they prefer.
